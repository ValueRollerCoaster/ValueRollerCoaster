## üéØ How It Works

### Step 1: Define Your Values
Your organization defines value components that represent what you offer. These aren't just marketing copy‚Äîthey're the real value your team delivers every day.

**How Customer Benefits Are Created:**
When you enter a value component (like "Certified manufacturing processes"), the app uses **AI-powered analysis** to transform it into a customer benefit. This process:

1. **Gathers company context** from your company profile‚Äîyour value proposition, success patterns, customer outcomes, and capabilities
2. **Selects relevant context** based on the component's category (Technical, Business, Strategic, or After Sales)
3. **Generates the benefit** using AI that considers your company's unique context and how you deliver value
4. **Validates quality** to ensure the benefit is factual, specific, and not just marketing fluff

**Example:**
- **Your Input**: "Certified manufacturing processes"
- **AI Considers**: Your company's focus on reliability, your success patterns, your customer outcomes
- **Customer Benefit Generated**: "Products meet international quality standards, reducing risk and ensuring compliance"

This isn't simple paraphrasing‚Äîit's a sophisticated transformation that connects your internal capabilities to customer-facing benefits using your company's actual value delivery context.

### Step 2: Generate Buyer Personas
Enter a prospect's website URL. The AI analyzes their needs, pain points, and goals, then **matches them with your value components**.

### Step 3: See the Alignment
Get a clear alignment matrix showing:
- Which of your values match their needs
- Match scores (0-100%) for each alignment
- Evidence from their website supporting each match
- Conversation starters ready to use

## ğŸ“– What Are Value Components?

Value components are the **structured way** your organization defines and organizes company values. Instead of abstract concepts on a wall, they become **tangible, actionable tools** that:

1. **Capture diverse perspectives** - Each employee interprets values from their role
2. **Transform into customer benefits** - AI converts inputs into customer-focused value
3. **Enable practical applications** - Use transformed values for better customer conversations

> **Important**: This structure is a **starting point**, not a limitation. It reflects B2B production company experience, but you can **fully customize** it to match your industry, business model, or unique value framework.

### When to Customize

Consider adapting the structure if you're in:
- **Different Industries**: SaaS, retail, healthcare, education, non-profit
- **Different Business Models**: Subscription-only, marketplace, platform, consulting
- **Different Value Focus**: Customer experience, community, social impact, innovation-driven

- **Adjust components** - Match your specific value propositions
- **Change weights** - Reflect your priorities

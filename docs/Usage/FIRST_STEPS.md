## Step 1: Company Profile Setup

**üë§ Who: Admins only**  
**üéØ Goal: Configure the app for your company**

### Why This Matters

The Company Profile customizes the entire application for your business:
- **AI Analysis**: All AI prompts adapt to your company
- **Industry Frameworks**: Frameworks generated for your industries
- **Value Components**: AI suggests values based on your business
- **Branding**: Your company name and colors throughout

### What You'll Do

1. **Access Company Profile**
   - Go to **üîß Admin Tools** in sidebar
   - Click **üè¢ Company Profile**
   - Click **‚úèÔ∏è Edit Company Profile** button

2. **Fill Company Information**
   - **Company Name** (required): Your official company name
   - **Company Size**: Select from dropdown
   - **Location Type** (required): How your company is organized
   - **Primary Location**: Main office or headquarters
   - **Core Business** (required): 2-3 sentences describing what you do

3. **Configure Target Market**
   - **Target Customers** (required): Select at least one customer type
   - **Industries Served** (required): Select at least one industry
   - **Tip**: Use AI suggestions if unsure
   - **Tip**: You can add custom options with AI validation

4. **Describe Products & Services**
   - **Products and Services** (required): List your main offerings
   - **Value Propositions**: What makes you unique (recommended)

5. **Set Business Intelligence**
   - Fill out all business intelligence fields (all required)
   - Be accurate - this affects AI analysis quality
   - Options include: Company Type, Business Model, Market Position, etc.

6. **Configure Branding** (Optional - never tested :)
   - **Primary Color**: Choose your brand color
   - **Logo URL**: Enter your logo URL (if you have one)

7. **Save Profile**
   - Click **Save Company Profile**
   - Wait for success message
   - Profile is now active!



## Step 2: Value Components (Critical)

**‚è±Ô∏è Time: 15-30 minutes**  
**üë§ Who: Everyone**  
**üéØ Goal: Define your organization's values (90% completion required)**

### Why This Matters

Value Components are the **foundation** of everything:
- **Required for Persona Generation**: You need 90%+ completion to generate personas
- **Value Alignment**: Used to match your values with prospect needs
- **AI Analysis**: AI uses your values to provide relevant insights
- **Living Values**: Makes abstract values into actionable tools

### What "90% Completion" Means

You need to fill out **at least 90% of all value component fields** across 4 categories:
- **Technical Value**: Quality, Performance, Innovation, Sustainability
- **Business Value**: Cost Savings, Revenue Growth, Efficiency Gains
- **Strategic Value**: Competitive Advantage, Risk Mitigation, Partnership Development
- **After Sales Value**: Customer Support, Maintenance, User Experience

**Each category has subcategories and components** - fill out most of them to reach 90%.

### What You'll Do

1. **Navigate to Value Components**
   - Click **üíé Value Components** in sidebar
   - You'll see a summary view with completion status

2. **Check Current Completion**
   - Look for completion percentage (should show in summary)
   - If below 90%, you need to add more values

3. **Choose Your Approach**

   **Option A: AI Generation (Faster)**
   - Click **‚ö° Generate Missing** to fill empty fields
   - Or click **üîÑ Regenerate All** to start fresh
   - AI uses your Company Profile to suggest values
   - **Time**: 5-10 minutes
   - **Best for**: Quick setup, getting started

   **Option B: Manual Entry (Recommended - goal of the app)**
   - Navigate through categories (Technical, Business, Strategic, After Sales)
   - Fill in each value component manually
   - Edit AI-generated benefits if needed
   - **Time**: 20-30 minutes
   - **Best for**: Precise control, specific values


4. **Fill Value Components**

   For each component:
   - **Enter Your Value**: Describe what you offer (be specific!)
   - **AI Generates Benefit**: Automatically creates customer-focused benefit
   - **Edit if Needed**: Modify the AI-generated benefit
   - **Save**: Changes save automatically

5. **Verify Completion**
   - Check completion percentage in summary
   - Should be **90% or higher**
   - If not, add more values or use AI generation

### Tips for Value Components

- **Be Specific**: "We deliver 99.9% uptime" not "We're reliable"
- **Focus on Customer**: Describe customer benefit, not just features
- **Use Real Examples**: Reference actual customer success stories
- **Review AI Output**: AI suggestions are starting points - customize them
- **Complete All Categories**: Don't skip categories - fill all 4

### Common Mistakes to Avoid

- ‚ùå **Too Generic**: "We're great" doesn't help - be specific
- ‚ùå **Skipping Categories**: Fill all 4 categories, not just one
- ‚ùå **Not Reaching 90%**: You need 90%+ to generate personas
- ‚ùå **Ignoring AI Suggestions**: AI can help - use it!

### ‚úÖ Checkpoint

After Step 2, you should have:
- [ ] Value Components at 90%+ completion
- [ ] All 4 categories have values
- [ ] Summary shows completion percentage
- [ ] Ready to generate personas

**Ready?** Move to Step 3!

## Step 3: Generate Your First Persona

**‚è±Ô∏è Time: 5-8 minutes (wait time)**  
**üë§ Who: Everyone**  
**üéØ Goal: Generate your first buyer persona**

### Why This Matters

This is where you see Value Rollercoaster in action:
- **AI Analysis**: Three AI models analyze a prospect website
- **Value Alignment**: See how your values match prospect needs
- **Evidence-Based**: Get website quotes supporting each match
- **Actionable Insights**: Ready-to-use conversation starters

### What You'll Do

1. **Navigate to Persona Generator**
   - Click **üë§ Persona Generator** in sidebar
   - You'll see the persona generation interface

2. **Choose a Test Prospect**
   - Use a real prospect you're working with

3. **Enter Website URL**
   - Paste the prospect's website URL
   - Include `https://` or `http://`
   - Example: `https://www.example-company.com`

4. **Select Industry** (Optional)
   - Choose industry from dropdown if available
   - Or leave blank - AI will detect it

5. **Start Generation**
   - Click **Generate Buyer Persona** button
   - Generation starts immediately
   - **Don't close the browser!** Wait for completion

6. **Watch Progress**
   - Progress is shown in sidebar
   - You'll see current step (e.g., "Analyzing website...")
   - Progress percentage updates
   - **Time**: 5-8 minutes total

### What Happens During Generation

The AI performs 7 steps with quality gates:

1. **üîç Dual-Model Website Analysis**: Two AI models analyze the website
2. **‚úÖ Cross-Model Validation**: Compare insights for accuracy
3. **üè≠ Enhanced Market Intelligence**: Gather industry context
4. **üéØ Dual-Model Value Alignment**: Match your values with their needs
5. **üé® Creative Persona Elements**: Generate persona details
6. **üß† Final Persona Synthesis**: Combine everything
7. **üîç Quality Assurance**: Final quality check

**9 quality checkpoints** ensure accuracy throughout!

### While You Wait

- **Don't Close Browser**: Keep the tab open
- **Don't Navigate Away**: Stay on the Persona Generator page
- **Be Patient**: 5-8 minutes is normal
- **Watch Progress**: Check sidebar for updates

### ‚úÖ Checkpoint

After Step 3, you should have:
- [ ] Persona generation completed
- [ ] Success message or persona displayed
- [ ] Ready to view results

## Understanding Your Results

### What You'll See

Once generation completes, you'll see **5 tabs** with detailed information:

#### Tab 1: Company Overview
- Company name, website, industry
- Products and services
- Business model
- Market position

#### Tab 2: Profile
- Pain points
- Goals and objectives
- Value drivers
- Strategic priorities

#### Tab 3: Value Alignment ‚≠ê **Most Important**
- **Alignment Matrix**: Shows how your values match their needs
- **Match Scores**: 0-100% for each alignment
- **Evidence**: Website quotes supporting each match
- **Conversation Starters**: Ready-to-use questions and talking points

#### Tab 4: Industry Context
- Market intelligence
- Industry trends
- Economic indicators
- Competitive landscape

#### Tab 5: AI Insights
- AI reasoning (chain of thought)
- Cross-model validation results
- Quality confidence scores
- Additional insights

### Focus on Value Alignment Matrix

This is the **most valuable output**:

- **High Scores (80-100%)**: Lead with these in conversations
- **Medium Scores (50-79%)**: Use as supporting points
- **Low Scores (0-49%)**: May indicate areas to develop or skip

**How to Use:**
1. Review alignment scores
2. Read the evidence (website quotes)
3. Use conversation starters in your next call
4. Reference specific matches in your pitch

### Save Your Persona

- Click **üíæ Save Persona** button
- Persona is saved to database
- Accessible in **Persona Search** tab later

### Understanding Confidence Scores

- **8-10**: High confidence, reliable results
- **5-7**: Medium confidence, use with validation
- **0-4**: Low confidence, do additional research

Find confidence scores in **Tab 5: AI Insights**.

- [ ] Know which values match best (high scores)
